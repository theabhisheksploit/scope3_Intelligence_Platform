# scope3_Intelligence_Platform

Problem statement:
Over 96% of JLL client emissions fall under Scope 3, yet no standard tool, baselines, or pathways to measure and reduce these emissions effectively. We are building the Scope 3 Intelligence Platform to solve the impossibility of measuring and verifying Scope 3 emissions at scale for JLL. Our AI-powered solution automates data extraction, category mapping, and calculations while maintaining full compliance with GHG Protocol standards.
