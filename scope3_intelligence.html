Emission Factor Reference Library

All emission factors used in this platform are sourced from the databases below, as recommended by the GHG Protocol Technical Guidance for Calculating Scope 3 Emissions. Every calculation result in the platform is traceable to one of these sources.

UK DEFRA GHG Conversion Factors 2023
Published annually by the UK Department for Environment, Food & Rural Affairs. The most widely used secondary EF source for transport (Cat 4, 6), waste (Cat 5), and energy. Covers road, rail, air, sea transport; waste disposal by type; and fuel combustion. Life cycle (well-to-wheel) factors used for Scope 3 per GHG Protocol requirements.
Cat 4 TransportCat 6 TravelCat 5 WasteCat 3 Energy
URL: gov.uk/government/collections/government-conversion-factors-for-company-reporting · Updated annually
GHG Protocol Emission Factor Database
Maintained by World Resources Institute / WBCSD. Cradle-to-gate process-based EFs for materials, metals, and manufactured goods. Primary source for Cat 1 average-data method (steel: 1.85 kgCO₂e/kg virgin, 0.46 recycled; aluminium: 8.24 kgCO₂e/kg). All EFs represent full upstream life cycle per GHG Protocol boundary requirements.
Cat 1 PG&SCat 2 Capital
URL: ghgprotocol.org/Third-Party-Databases · Version 2022
Exiobase 3 — Environmentally-Extended Input-Output (EEIO) Database
Multi-regional EEIO database covering 44 countries and 5 rest-of-world regions. Used exclusively as the spend-based (last-resort) method per GHG Protocol Fig 1.2 hierarchy when physical activity data is unavailable. Output: kgCO₂e per unit of monetary spend by sector. Limitation per GHG Protocol: broad sector averages, cannot distinguish intra-sector product differences, indicative only.
Cat 1 spend-based fallbackCat 2 spend-based fallback
Exiobase 3.8.2 · 2021 edition · exiobase.eu
IEA Electricity Grid Emission Factors 2023
International Energy Agency country-level grid emission factors for electricity generation. Used for Cat 3 (fuel and energy-related activities) upstream electricity emissions. India grid EF: 0.82 kgCO₂e/kWh (2023). Covers generation mix and T&D losses per GHG Protocol scope 3 boundary for Cat 3.
Cat 3 Energy
IEA Emissions Factors 2023 · iea.org/data-and-statistics
IPCC AR6 GWP100 Values (2021)
Global Warming Potential values from the IPCC Sixth Assessment Report, 100-year time horizon. Used to convert all non-CO₂ greenhouse gases to CO₂ equivalent. Key values: CH₄ = 27.9 (fossil), N₂O = 273, HFC-134a = 1,526. Replaces AR5 values per latest GHG Protocol guidance.
All categories — GWP conversion
IPCC AR6 WGI Chapter 7 Supplementary Material · 2021
ICAO Carbon Emissions Calculator
International Civil Aviation Organization methodology for air transport emissions. Used for Cat 4 and Cat 6 air freight and business travel EFs by route and aircraft class. Includes Radiative Forcing Index (RFI = 1.9) applied to air travel per GHG Protocol recommendation for non-CO₂ climate effects at altitude.
Cat 4 Air freightCat 6 Air travel
ICAO Doc 9889 · icao.int/environmental-protection
EcoTransIT World / NTM
Transport-specific emission factor databases cited in GHG Protocol Technical Guidance Table 4.2 for road, rail, and multimodal freight. Used as secondary source for truck and rail EFs where DEFRA does not cover specific vehicle types or non-UK routes.
Cat 4 Road/Rail
ecotransit.org · ntmcalc.se
Important limitation: This platform currently embeds representative EF values from the above databases (last updated at platform build time). For production reporting, connect a live EF API (Climatiq, Sphera) to ensure factors are always current. GHG Protocol requires disclosure of EF source, year, and geography for each calculation — this is captured in the audit trail on the Reporting tab.
